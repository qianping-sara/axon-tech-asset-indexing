### Consolidated Servicing Business Domains & Capabilities

#### Core Servicing Domains (5)
These domains represent the primary value-delivering processes that directly fulfill insurance service promises to customers.

| Domain | Description | Example Sub-capabilities / Processes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Claim** | Manages all customer requests arising from an insured event, fulfilling the core promise of the insurance contract. | * First Notice of Loss (FNOL)<br>* Case Investigation & Assessment<br>* Claim Adjudication & Calculation<br>* Claim Payout |
| **Financial Change** | Handles changes related to the financial attributes of a policy, focusing on policy maintenance, corrections, and status. | * Arrear Premium Handling<br>* Policy Revival / Reinstatement<br>* Refund Processing<br>* Payment Method / Frequency Change |
| **Inquiry & General Changes** | Responds to routine customer queries and processes non-financial updates to policyholder information. | * Policy Information Inquiry<br>* Service Status Tracking<br>* Contact / Address Change<br>* Beneficiary Update |
| **Money Out (Value Withdrawal)** | Processes all non-claim related fund withdrawals initiated by the customer or as scheduled by the contract. | * Divestment / Partial Withdrawal<br>* Annuity Payout<br>* Maturity Payout<br>* Policy Loan |
| **Wealth** | Provides specialized services related to investment-linked or wealth management products (e.g., ILP, Universal Life). | * Investment Portfolio Inquiry<br>* Asset Allocation Change<br>* Fund Switch<br>* Net Asset Value (NAV) Notification |

#### Support Domain Capabilities (5)
These domains provide cross-cutting support services that enable and enhance core servicing processes.

| Domain | Description | Example Sub-capabilities / Processes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Customer Engagement** | Ensures a consistent, seamless, and personalized service experience across all customer touchpoints and manages proactive customer communications. | * Unified Customer View (360)<br>* Cross-Channel Consistency<br>* Unified Authentication (UAW/SSO)<br>* Customer Journey Management<br>* Multi-Channel Notifications (SMS, Email, Letter)<br>* Communication Template Management<br>* Preference Management |
| **Customer & Relationship Management** | Manages the master data and single source of truth for customer information, enabling personalized service delivery. | * Create/Update Customer Profile<br>* Retrieve Customer Details<br>* Customer Identification & Verification (CIF)<br>* Relationship Tracking<br>* Customer Segmentation |
| **Payment & Settlement** | Provides the underlying infrastructure for all fund movements (inflows and outflows) supporting business processes. | * Payment Gateway Integration<br>* Direct Debit Authority (DDA/Giro)<br>* Claims & Refund Payouts<br>* Electronic Receipts |
| **Finance & Accounting** | Manages all financial bookkeeping, reconciliation, and settlement generated by service activities to ensure accuracy. | * General Ledger (GL)<br>* Accounts Receivable / Payable (AR/AP)<br>* Bank Reconciliation<br>* Financial Reporting |
| **Risk & Compliance** | Ensures all service processes adhere to regulatory requirements (Regulatory) and internal risk controls (Risk). | * Anti-Money Laundering (AML)<br>* Know Your Customer (KYC)<br>* Operational Risk Monitoring<br>* Regulatory Reporting |

#### General Domain Capabilities (1)
These domains provide fundamental infrastructure, utilities, and platform services that support all other domains across the organization.

| Domain | Description | Example Sub-capabilities / Processes |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Common capabilities** | Provides shared infrastructure, data management, identity services, and document management capabilities that enable all business and support domains to operate effectively. | * Master Data Management (MDM)<br>* Data Quality Monitoring<br>* Data Integration & ETL<br>* Analytics & Reporting Infrastructure<br>* Single Sign-On (SSO)<br>* User Authentication & Authorization<br>* Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)<br>* Document Storage & Archival<br>* Document Retrieval & Search<br>* Document Lifecycle Management |
